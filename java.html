Investigations

Apollo HomeCare is equipped to send home health care providers, healthcare specialists and technicians to conduct investigations and collect lab samples from patients from within the comfort of their homes.

Below are some of the common investigations done at home

  • Complete Blood Picture
  • Blood Sugar levels
  • HbA1C levels
  • Prothrombin time & INR
  • Liver function tests
  • Kidney function tests
  • Thyroid function tests
  • Cardiac markers- tr
  • Arterial blood gases-ABG
  • Serum electrolytes
  • ECG
  • Urinanalysis
